Tony D. Trucking LLC, is a full service transportation, partner to businesses across the north east United States.

With over four years of experience, Tony D. Trucking LLC has always been punctual and reasonably priced. Our safety department ensures that our drivers are in compliance with FMCSA and DOT regulations, securing the safety of your freight.

We empower our customers with the tools needed to manage their total transportation needs efficiently. We are committed to supply your business with flexible and dependable service.

Our company specializes in same day delivery, next day delivery, liftgate, air ride and roller bed service, uncrating, and two to three man deliveries. Throughout the years, we have run our business with a strong commitment to our customer needs.

We offer low cost solutions for your ground freight shipping without sacrificing speedy and cordial service. From local to long-distance deliveries, hot shot to regular service, Tony D. Trucking offers our full range of vehicles to cater to the size of freight or shipment.

Our customer service will always treat you with the utmost respect and cooperation. For any of your needs, call us 7 days a week.
